[h1]Break Timer[/h1]

A RimWorld mod that shows how much time is left on a pawn's [b]mental break[/b] and lists every mental break a pawn is currently at risk of. See what's coming, where it comes from, and when it will pass.

[h2]What it shows[/h2]
To the left of the pawn's mood bar you'll see a lightning bolt. Mouse over to get:
[list]
[*]The mood breaks a pawn is currently at risk of.
[*]How much time is left once a pawn is mid-break.
[*]Breaks caused by hediffs.
[*]Breaks caused by pawn state (such as being a baby).
[/list]
